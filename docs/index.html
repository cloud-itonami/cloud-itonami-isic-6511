Life Insurance Blueprint

ISIC Rev.5 6511
The problem: LLMs can draft underwriting checklists and flag incomplete applications, but they have no jurisdiction to know what insurance regulations actually require, no license to underwrite, and no business deciding whether to bind real coverage. Building your own from scratch invites regulatory risk.
The solution: An independent UnderwritingGovernor seals the Underwriter-LLM inside a governed workflow. The LLM drafts; the Governor checks jurisdiction facts (spec-cited), screens against KYC/sanctions, validates documents, and gates policy binding. A licensed human underwriter always makes the final call. Every decision is recorded in an immutable audit ledger.
What this is not: This repository does not hold a license to underwrite insurance in any jurisdiction. Your organization (licensed insurer, MGA, employer) supplies the jurisdiction, the KYC/AML program, and the liability. The software supplies the governed, audited scaffold so you don't rebuild compliance from scratch.
