Life Insurance Blueprint

ISIC Rev.5 6511

An open-source, governed life-insurance underwriting actor for licensed operators. This actor drafts underwriting proposals, screens applicants against KYC/AML requirements, and enforces jurisdictional compliance—all sealed inside an independent UnderwritingGovernor that ensures no policy is bound without a human licensed underwriter's approval.

Built on a portable ClojureScript actor runtime with an immutable audit ledger, this blueprint is designed for mutual insurers, community mutual-aid societies, employers administering group life plans, and licensed independent insurers who want to self-host instead of buying closed SaaS.

The Underwriter-LLM advisor drafts; the UnderwritingGovernor checks jurisdiction facts, KYC/sanctions hits, and document completeness; only a licensed human binds real coverage.
